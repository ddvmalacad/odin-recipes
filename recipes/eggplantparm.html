Air Fryer Eggplant Parmesan Recipe

Description

Using an air fryer, you can enjoy crispy, crunchy eggplant Parmesan rounds without any oil. They come out perfectly golden and satisfying every time.

Whether you enjoy them plain or layered with marinara sauce and gooey mozzarella cheese, they make a flavorful and satisfying dish.

These eggplant rounds are not only easy to make but also a healthier alternative to traditional fried versions.

Ingredients

This is directly from "allrecipes.com". The ingredients used for this recipe are for 4 servings

  • ½ cup Italian bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 medium eggplant, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 1 cup marinara sauce, or more to taste
  • 8 slices mozzarella cheese, or as needed
