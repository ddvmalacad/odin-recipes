Miso Soup Recipe

Description

This miso soup is rich in umami flavor.

Use more dashi for a stronger broth, and choose white, yellow, or red miso depending on your taste—white is mild, yellow is balanced, and red is bold and salty.

Ingredients

This is directly from "Allrecipes.com". The ingredients used for this recipe is for 4 servings

  • 1 (1 pound) rib eye steak
  • salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 Tablespoons garlic and herb salted butter (such as Kerrygold®), plus more for serving (optional)

Steps

This is directly from "Allrecipes.com"

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.
  4. Stir in tofu.
  5. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup. Simmer gently for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
